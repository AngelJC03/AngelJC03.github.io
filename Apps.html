Angel's Programming Portfolio

For my final project in CSC 106, we were assigned a task of creating a program for high school students to use to demonstrate our knowledge of the Khan Javascript. In the program, we were expected to use certain terms we learned about during the course, such as variables, functions, object classes, loops, and arrays. The program is called Log Rush, a game where you are assigned a boat character and are to dodge all incoming logs and every 100 logs, the speed increases as the level increases.
